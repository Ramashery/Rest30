Our Story

Founded in 2010, Aquarella brings the authentic flavors of Italy to Tbilisi. Our chef, trained in the culinary traditions of Tuscany, crafts each dish with passion and the finest ingredients imported directly from Italy.

We believe in the art of slow dining - where every meal is an experience to be savored. Our elegant ambiance, paired with impeccable service, creates the perfect setting for your special occasions.
